Cohesion and Coupling •In general, there's a balance to be made between low coupling and high cohesion in your designs. •For a complex system, the complexity can be distributed to between the modules or within the modules. •As modules are simplified to achieve high cohesion, they may need to depend more on other modules thus increasing coupling. •As connections between modules are simplified to achieve low coupling, the modules may need to take on more responsibilities thus lowering cohesion
